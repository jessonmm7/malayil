Who We Are

Established in the year 1994, Malayil Food Park successfully caters to superior quality food products like rice, spices, flour, Pickles, and different types of pulses, etc... to our clients. These products are widely marketed in Kerala and nearby states, and are exported to other countries.

We are flourishing day by day, under the able veneration of C H Mohammed Gaddafi. He believes in hard work and consistency and advocates that one should always make the best efforts. He has immense experience in this industry that has helped us in attaining a stiff and recognized position in today's highly competitive world.

We observe high-quality standards during the manufacturing and packing of our products, and our products are kept at specific temperatures to avoid moisture and other microbiological defects, and the products are mixed in a specific proportion to satisfy customers' tastes and according to India's food safety standards. Raw materials are handled with care to protect from all kinds of defects, and We get our raw materials directly from the farms and centres.

Our robust infrastructure coupled with a talented team of technicians and the production team ensures that we manufacture food products commensurate to international standards. We lay special emphasis on the packaging of our products, so they reach their destination in the best condition without losing their nutrition, taste, texture, and aroma.

Today, the brand has carved a niche in the minds of millions of Malayalee’s around the world by selecting, processing, and, above all, making it affordable, delivering the traditional tastes. Our focus is always on customer satisfaction and we follow quality business policies and transparent transactions. We also conduct a variety of quality checks on our products before they are delivered to our clients. Our products are one of the finest in India and are processed using strict adherence to world-class machinery and the highest nutritional and hygienic standards.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide creative, high-tech, high-quality, safe, and nutritious fresh goods of excellence to satisfy customer needs, provide permanent well-being and quality of life, as well as environmental health.

Our Vision

Be the number one food product enterprise in the minds of consumers, known by the consistency and variety of safe items we sell. Have highly qualified workers maintaining strict and stringent quality management and reducing our impact on the environment via life plan.

Delivering the natural taste of Indian spices globally since 1994. Quality, purity, and tradition in every pinch.
